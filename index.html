SLIPSTREAM: PORTAL 2P Strategy

▶ Play
Tagline: Three ships. One portal. Zero room for error.

SLIPSTREAM is a high-skill, turn-based space strategy game where you command three UAPs (Unified Astral Phantoms) in a desperate race to reach the portal. On your turn, position your fleet in formation and shoot one into action—scattering your ships across the void. Thread ships perfectly through the gap to keep your turn alive. First to five points wins.

Core Features
  • Turn-based “shoot & thread” skill strategy
  • Three-ship formation puzzle each turn
  • Five unique UAP characters with special abilities
  • Anchor mechanic for stop/ricochet points
  • Fast matches designed for rematches
Tone / Controls / Status
Tone: Competitive precision—easy to learn, hard to master.
Controls: In-game on-screen notes (varies by build).
Status: Playable prototype.

I’M B.O.S.S.Y! Life Strategy

▶ Play
Tagline: Balance your life. Organize your moves. Build something stable. Stay safe.

I’M B.O.S.S.Y! is a competitive life-strategy game where players manage real-world responsibilities in a 12-month cycle. Earn chips through work, upgrade the quality of your food, home, utilities, and morale, and allocate money between your wallet, savings, and investments. Keep your required life categories at Medium or higher to earn streaks—first to 3 streaks wins.

Core Features
  • Tier-based life management (None / Low / Medium / High)
  • 12-month rent & utilities tracker
  • Wallet, savings, investments & loans
  • Streak system: earn streaks through stability
  • 2–4 players, strategy + planning
Tone / Controls / Status
Tone: Competitive, strategic, real-life themed.
Controls: In-game on-screen notes (varies by build).
Status: Playable prototype / board-game-to-digital build.
